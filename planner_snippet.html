🧩 Build your own itinerary

Drag any activity into a day slot — on a phone, tap the activity, then tap a slot. Your plan saves automatically on this device, and Copy share link gives you a URL that opens your exact plan on anyone's device. Tip: click a slot that already has a stop in it and the Attractions & Eats tabs color-code 🟢🟡🔴 by how close everything is to that stop. Every day also has a ☔ rain plan tab — stage an indoor backup (use the ☔ indoor only filter) and it shows up as Plan B in the day-by-day above. Cost tags are rough, per person: $ pocket change → $$$$$ big-ticket; no tag = free.
